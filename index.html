About Us

PetFinder is a community-driven platform dedicated to reuniting lost pets with their families. Our mission is to provide a safe, easy-to-use space where pet owners and compassionate individuals can connect to help pets find their way home. Every pet deserves to be with their loved ones, and we're here to make that happen.
